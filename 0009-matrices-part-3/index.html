Multiplying two matrices
Multiplication two Matrices
Matrix $M$ ($r\times n$) can multiply matrix $N$ ($n\times c$).
The result $MN$ is $r\times c$.
Example:
3x2 matrix multiply by 2x4
$ \left[ \begin{smallmatrix} m_{11} & m_{12} \\ m_{21} & m_{22} \\ m_{31} & m_{32} \end{smallmatrix} \right] \left[ \begin{smallmatrix} n_{11} & n_{12} & n_{13} & n_{14} \\ n_{21} & n_{22} & n_{23} & n_{24}\end{smallmatrix} \right] $
Example:
Result is 3x4
$ \left[ \begin{smallmatrix} mn_{11} & mn_{12} & mn_{13} & mn_{14} \\ mn_{21} & mn_{22} & mn_{23} & mn_{24} \\ mn_{31} & mn_{32} & mn_{33} & mn_{34} \end{smallmatrix} \right] $
Each element of $MN$ (we'll use c instead of mn),
is the vector dot product of the row $i$ of M with the column $j$ of N:
$ c_{ij} =\sum_{i=1}^n a_{ik}b_{kj} $
Example:
$ \left[ \begin{smallmatrix} m_{11} & m_{12} \\ m_{21} & m_{22} \\ m_{31} & m_{32} \end{smallmatrix} \right] \left[ \begin{smallmatrix} n_{11} & n_{12} & n_{13} & n_{14} \\ n_{21} & n_{22} & n_{23} & n_{24}\end{smallmatrix} \right] = \left[ \begin{smallmatrix} c_{11} & c_{12} & c_{13} & c_{14} \\ c_{21} & c_{22} & c_{23} & c_{24} \\ c_{31} & c_{32} & c_{33} & c_{34} \end{smallmatrix} \right] $
Example:
$\left[ \begin{smallmatrix} n_{11} & n_{12} & n_{13} & n_{14} \\ n_{21} & n_{22} & n_{23} & n_{24}\end{smallmatrix} \right]$

$ \left[ \begin{smallmatrix} m_{11} & m_{12} \\ m_{21} & m_{22} \\ m_{31} & m_{32} \end{smallmatrix} \right] = \left[ \begin{smallmatrix} c_{11} & c_{12} & c_{13} & c_{14} \\ c_{21} & c_{22} & c_{23} & c_{24} \\ c_{31} & c_{32} & c_{33} & c_{34} \end{smallmatrix} \right] $
$c_{31} = m_{31}n_{11} + m_{32}n_{21}$
$c_{23} = m_{21}n_{13} + m_{22}n_{23}$
Example:
$ \left[ \begin{smallmatrix} m_{11} & m_{12} & m_{13} \\ m_{21} & m_{22} & m_{23} \\ m_{31} & m_{32} & m_{33} \end{smallmatrix} \right] \left[ \begin{smallmatrix} n_{11} & n_{12} & n_{13} \\ n_{21} & n_{22} & n_{23} \\ n_{31} & n_{32} & n_{33} \end{smallmatrix} \right] $ =

$ \left[ \begin{smallmatrix} m_{11}n_{11}+m_{12}n_{21}+m_{13}n_{31} & m_{11}n_{12}+m_{12}n_{22}+m_{13}n_{32} & m_{11}n_{13}+m_{12}n_{23}+m_{13}n_{33} \\ m_{21}n_{11}+m_{22}n_{21}+m_{23}n_{31} & m_{21}n_{12}+m_{22}n_{22}+m_{23}n_{32} & m_{21}n_{13}+m_{22}n_{23}+m_{23}n_{33} \\ m_{31}n_{11}+m_{32}n_{21}+m_{33}n_{31} & m_{31}n_{12}+m_{32}n_{22}+m_{33}n_{32} & m_{31}n_{13}+m_{32}n_{23}+m_{33}n_{33} \end{smallmatrix} \right] $
Notes
  • $MI = IM = M$
  • Not commutative: $MN \neq NM$
  • Associative: $(MN)O = M(NO)$
  • Associates with vector/scalar: $(kM)N = k(MN) = M(kN)$ and $(vM)N = v(MN)$
  • Transpose of two matrices = product of transposes in reverse: $(AB)^T = B^TA^T$
